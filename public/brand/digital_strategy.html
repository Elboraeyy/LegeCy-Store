LegaCy

Digital Architecture Strategy

"The Digital Concierge": Only necessary interactions. Friction is the enemy of luxury.

1. UX Philosophy

Invisible UI

Interface recedes. Imagery is hero. Buttons appear only when needed.

Anticipatory

Don't make users hunt. "Add to Cart" always within reach.

Click Value

No dead ends. Every click rewards the user with details.

2. Visual Strategy

The Homepage

One Hero Video: Slow motion, mute. Single button "Discover".
Scroll Sequence: Essentials > Philosophy > Journal.

The Product Page (PDP)

Gallery: Vertical scroll, large uncropped images.
Sticky Buy Box: Title, Price, and "ACQUIRE" button always visible.

The Checkout

The Private Room: No header/footer. Pure focus on transaction.

3. Mobile Experience

Thumb Zone

Buttons 48px min height. Bottom anchored for easy reach.

Tab Bar

App-like navigation. Stop using Hamburger menus if possible.

4. Guidelines

DO
Use high-res video backgrounds. Show product in context.
DO
Use serif fonts sparingly for prolonged reading only.
DON'T
Use "Sale" pop-ups. It immediately cheapens the brand.
DON'T
Use aggressive countdown timers or auto-play sound.
