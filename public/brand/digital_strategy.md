# LegaCy Phase 4: Digital Architecture Strategy

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **UX MANDATE**: The LegaCy digital experience is not an "Online Store". It is a "Digital Boutique". It must feel like entering a quiet, carpeted room where a concierge knows your name. **Friction is the enemy of luxury.**

---

## 1. UX Philosophy: "The Digital Concierge"

### Core Principles
1.  **Invisible UI**: The interface should recede. Product imagery is the hero. Buttons and menus appear only when needed.
2.  **Anticipatory Service**: Don't make the user hunt. If they are on a product page, the "Add to Cart" button is always reachable.
3.  **Click Value**: Every click must result in a significant reward (a beautiful image, a key detail). No dead ends.

### Visual Architecture
*   **The Canvas**: Primary backgrounds are **Deep Green (#12403C)** for immersion or **Off-White (#F5F5F5)** for readability (Blogs/Legal).
*   **The Ink**: Text is **Gold (#F6E5C6)** on dark, **Black (#111)** on light.
*   **The Structure**: Strict Grid. High margins. The website breathes.

---

## 2. Website Visual Strategy

### A. Global Elements
*   **Navigation**:
    *   *Desktop*: Minimal top bar. "Shop", "Philosophy", "Vault" (Account). Centered Logo.
    *   *Mobile*: Bottom Tab Bar (App-like feel). Thumb-reachable.
*   **Micro-Interactions**:
    *   *Hover*: Subtle lift (Y-axis -2px). Gold glow.
    *   *Click*: Solid tactile feedback (Button depression).
    *   *Loaders*: No spinning circles. A pulsing Gold 'L' or a filling horizontal line.

### B. The Homepage (The Storefront Window)
*   **Above the Fold**: ONE Hero Video (Slow motion, silent). No text overload. One button: "Discover".
*   **The Scroll**:
    *   **Section 1**: "The Essentials" (Horizontal scroll of top 3 products).
    *   **Section 2**: "The Philosophy" (Quote on Black).
    *   **Section 3**: "The Journal" (Lifestyle imagery).
*   **Goal**: Brand Immersion. Not immediate sales.

### C. The Product Page (The Inspection)
*   **Gallery**: Vertical scroll (Left side). Large, uncropped images.
*   **Buy Box**: Sticky (Right side).
    *   *Title*: Great Vibes (Accent) + Montserrat (Name).
    *   *Price*: Montserrat Regular. Small.
    *   *CTA*: Full width Gold button. Black Text. "ACQUIRE".
*   **Details**: Accordion menus for "Dimensions", "Materials", "Care". Keep the page short.

### D. The Checkout (The Transaction)
*   **Philosophy**: "The Private Room".
*   **UI**:
    *   Remove Header/Footer navigation. Focus only on the form.
    *   Progress Bar: "Shipping > Payment > Confirmation".
    *   Input Fields: Dark styling (Dark Grey background, Gold Text).
*   **Trust Signals**: Small Lock Icon (Gold). "Secure Encrypted Transaction" text.

## 3. E-mail Design System

### A. Transactional (The Receipt)
*   **Subject Line**: "Order #12345 Confirmed."
*   **Design**: Dark Header (Logo). Light Body (Clean Table).
*   **Vibe**: Official, precise, reassuring.

### B. Marketing (The Note)
*   **Subject Line**: Personal. "Regarding your collection." / "A moment of time."
*   **Design**: Plain Text aesthetic. No heavy image grids.
*   **Signature**: "The Concierge".
*   **Vibe**: A letter from a friend/advisor.

---

## 4. Mobile Experience rules

### Thumb Zone Design
*   **Buttons**: Height 48px minimum. Full width. Bottom anchored.
*   **Navigation**: Hamburger menus are a last resort. Use a bottom bar or horizontal scroll for categories.
*   **Images**: 4:5 Aspect Ratio (Portrait) to fill the phone screen.

### App Visual Direction (Future Proof)
*   **Biometrics**: "Unlock The Vault" (FaceID intro).
*   **Iconography**: Thin stroke icons (1px). Gold.
*   **Gestures**: Swipe Right to "Favorite". Long Press to "Examine" (Zoom).

---

## 5. Conversion Logic (CRO)

### The "Velvet Rope" Technique
*   **Scarcity**: "Limited Availability" (Real stock levels).
*   **Exclusivity**: "Members Only" access for new drops.
*   **Speed**: One-tap checkout (Apple Pay / Google Pay) is mandatory.

---

## 6. Do's & Don'ts

### DO
*   **Do**: Use high-resolution video backgrounds (compressed efficiently).
*   **Do**: Show the product in context (on a wrist, on a table) AND in isolation.
*   **Do**: Use a serif font (e.g., Cormorant Garamond) *sparingly* for editorial blog posts if needed for readability, but default to Montserrat.

### DON'T
*   **Don't**: Use "Sale" pop-ups. It cheapens the brand immediately.
*   **Don't**: Use aggressive countdown timers.
*   **Don't**: Auto-play sound.
