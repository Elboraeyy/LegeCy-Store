LegaCy

Packaging & Unboxing Architecture
MANUFACTURING DIRECTIVE: Deviations in material weight, color accuracy (Pantone 871C / #12403C), or finishing quality are unacceptable.

1. Strategy Overview

The Philosophy: "The Vault"

Packaging is a sanctuary. Concept: Hidden, heavy, private.

  • Weight: Heavier than expected.
  • Silence: Smooth friction openings.
  • Reveal: Slow and deliberate.

Hierarchy

  1. Primary: Rigid Box (High Touch)
  2. Secondary: Dust Bags/Tissue (Shield)
  3. Transport: Shipping/Shopping (Utility)

2. Primary Product Specs

A. The Watch Box (Flagship)

4. The Unboxing Flow

  1. Transport: Discreet Kraft Box. Security implied.
  2. Breach: Cutting the Gold/Green tape.
  3. Anticipation: Tissue cloud + Thank You card.
  4. Discovery: Breaking the Gold Seal.
  5. Encounter: The Heavy Rigid Box.
  6. Climax: Suction opening. Leather smell.

Do's & Don'ts

DO

  • Shake Test (No rattling)
  • Match Foil to Hardware
  • Hidden Magnets only

DON'T

  • No Glossy Lamination
  • No Logo on Box Bottom
  • No White Edges on wraps
